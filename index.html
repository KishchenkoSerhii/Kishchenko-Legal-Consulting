About Law firm "KLC"

The main specialization of KLC is legal support of non-profit activities of foreign organizations in Ukraine,

including NGOs and unions, charitable organizations, international technical assistance projects,

representative offices of foreign NGOs in Ukraine, etc.

In this capacity, KLC established itself as a reliable and professional assistant in resolving any

legal issues for non-profit organizations. For the last 11 years, we’ve been providing legal consultations advice for

UNDP, EU, American, and Canadian-funded international technical assistance and Ukrainian NGO's as well. 

Our services

Comprehensive support of the activities of international technical assistance projects implemented by non-resident organizations;

Provision of clarifications on the tax regime of ITA projectsand the specifics of the use of VAT benefits;

Labour relations with employees involved in the implementation of ITA projects, obtaining residence and/or employment permits for foreign employees, etc.;

Interpretation of the provisions of the current legislation of Ukraine, including tax legislation, by preparing legal opinions and obtaining clarifications from the authorized state bodies
